Comparing State Income and Obesity

Mouseover a state to learn more

As is apparent in the above chart, there is an observably strong, negative relationship between a state's income and the proportion of obesity in the population of that state.

This makes sense, as those with higher incomes likely have access to healthier food, better medical attention, and other factors which would benefit health.
